Jessy Ceha, PhD
(she/her)

        

Trained in both cognitive science and human-computer interaction, my research has ranged from EEG studies on speech perception with cochlear implants to social robots as educational tools. I hold a Ph.D. in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Waterloo, advised by Dr. Edith Law, an M.Sc. in Computational Cognitive Science (formerly Human-Machine Communication), from the University of Groningen, advised by Dr. Marieke van Vugt and Dr. Deniz Başkent, and a B.Sc. in Cognitive Systems, from the University of British Columbia.
